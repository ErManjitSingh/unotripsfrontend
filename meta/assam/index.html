Nestled in the heart of northeastern India, Assam is a land of extraordinary beauty — where lush tea gardens stretch to the horizon, the mighty Brahmaputra River carves through fertile plains, and one-horned rhinoceroses roam freely in Kaziranga National Park.

Experience the vibrant colours of the Bihu festival, explore ancient temples of Kamakhya, discover Majuli — the world's largest river island — and sip fresh tea straight from the garden.
